## Advanced Features Scope
1. **Multi-Tier Role Management**: Super Admin, Event Coordinator, Student. Position assignment (e.g., "Tech Lead", "Cultural Head").
2. **Event Management Suite**: Full CRUD (Create, Read, Edit, Delete), status tagging (Upcoming, Ongoing, Completed, Cancelled).
3. **Data Export & Roster Control**: Export participant lists to CSV/Excel and PDF print rosters. Attendance tracking (QR & manual check-in).
4. **Campus Announcements**: Public announcement ticker & bulletin board.
5. **Analytics & Insights**: Analytics dashboard with registration trends, category breakdowns, capacity metrics.
6. **User Account Controls**: Promote/demote users, assign positions, reset credentials, deactivate accounts.
7. **Value-Add Modules**:
   - Auto-generated Printable Certificates of Participation.
   - 5-Star Feedback & Rating System.
   - Google Calendar (.ics) Sync.
   - Post-Event Photo Gallery.
   - Dark Mode & Theme Switcher.
